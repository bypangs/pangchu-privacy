1. Information We Collect

Depending on the service used, PangChu Studio may collect limited information necessary to operate, maintain, and improve its website and games.

  • Basic gameplay-related data, such as progress, scores, rankings, settings, or in-game activity.
  • Technical information, such as device type, operating system, crash logs, or diagnostic data.
  • Account-related identifiers if sign-in or cloud save features are provided.
  • Purchase or entitlement-related records when in-app purchases are used.

PangChu Studio does not intentionally collect more personal information than reasonably necessary for the operation of the service.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the website, game services, cloud save, login, and leaderboards.
  • To maintain game functionality, fix bugs, and improve stability and performance.
  • To detect abuse, fraud, unauthorized access, or technical issues.
  • To manage purchases, entitlements, and service-related support.

3. Third-Party Services

The game uses the following third-party services: Google AdMob (advertisements), Unity Authentication and Unity Cloud Save (account login and cloud save), and Firebase Crashlytics (crash diagnostics). These services may collect limited technical data as described in their respective privacy policies.

Firebase Analytics is not currently in use. No behavioral analytics data is collected through Firebase at this time.

4. Data Retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, or maintain records related to game functionality and account history.

5. Children's Privacy

Requests may be reviewed and handled subject to legal obligations, technical feasibility, and verification needs.

8. Changes to This Policy

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact PangChu Studio through the official contact channel provided on the website or app listing.
