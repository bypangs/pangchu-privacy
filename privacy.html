Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-01

This Privacy Policy applies to PangChu Jump.

PangChu Jump ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how that information is used.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require users to create an account or submit personal information directly within the App.

The App may store limited gameplay and settings data locally on the user's device, such as:

  • Coin balance
  • Best score
  • Selected character skin
  • Selected visual effect
  • Sound settings such as BGM and SFX on/off
  • World unlock and other gameplay progress data

2. How Information Is Used

The locally stored data is used only to provide core gameplay features and save player preferences, including:

  • Saving game progress and unlocked content
  • Restoring selected skins, effects, and sound settings
  • Displaying scores, rewards, and game progression
  • Operating the game properly on the user's device

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade users' personal information.

Based on the current version of the App, we do not directly collect or transmit personal data to our own servers through the App.

However, Google Play, Android, payment providers, crash reporting tools, analytics tools, ad networks, or other platform services may process technical information as needed under their own policies if such services are used now or in the future.

4. Children's Privacy

PangChu Jump may be used by children. The App does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through in-app forms, account registration, or similar features.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so that we can review the issue.

5. Data Retention

Gameplay and settings data stored by the App generally remains on the user's device until it is deleted by the user, the app data is cleared, or the App is uninstalled.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the App functioning safely. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

The current or future version of the App may include third-party services such as Google Play services, in-app purchases, advertisements, analytics, crash reporting, cloud save, or sign-in features.

If those services are added or changed, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

8. Your Choices

You may stop all collection of locally stored gameplay data by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data through your device settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
